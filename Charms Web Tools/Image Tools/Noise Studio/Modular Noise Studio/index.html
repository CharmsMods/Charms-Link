Site Manual

Welcome to Digital Grain Studio

Digital Grain Studio is a GPU-accelerated image processing tool designed for high-performance visual effects, color grading, and noise generation.

Getting Started

  • Load an Image: Click the "LOAD IMAGE" button (📂) in the top-left to select a single picture to edit.
  • Batch Processing: Use "LOAD FOLDER" (🗂️) to process an entire directory. Navigate images using the playback controls or scrubber below it.
  • Activate Effects: The left panel contains all your layers (Adjustments, Noise, Blur, etc.). Check the box on any layer tab to enable it, then expand it to tweak the sliders.

Key Features

  • Layer Order: Click the "Render Layer Order" tab at the top left to drag and drop your active effects, changing how they apply to the image sequentially.
  • Realtime Analysis: Expand the top section to view live Luminance Histograms and a Vectorscope of your current image state.
  • High-Quality Preview: By default, previews are clamped to 2048px to save GPU memory. Toggle "High Quality Preview" on powerful systems to view effects at the source resolution.

Viewing & Navigating

  • Hover Zoom: Move your mouse over the main preview on the right to magnify details. Use the mouse wheel to adjust the zoom level.
  • Lock Zoom: Press TAB while hovering to lock the zoomed view in place.
  • Lens Mode: Click "FULL" above the preview to toggle it to a circular "LENS" magnifier.
  • Original Comparison: When not zoomed in, hover off the main canvas to briefly flash the original, unedited image for comparison.

Saving & Exporting

  • Save Settings (.JSON): Use the "SAVE .JSON" button to store your current slider values and layer order as a preset file. Check "Include Image" to bundle the photo inside the preset.
  • Exporting: Click "DOWNLOAD FULL RES" at the bottom of the left panel to render and download your final image. If you loaded a folder, this becomes a batch export option.

Press the Close button or click outside this window to return to the studio.
